About Me

Joshua Cruz is a 18-year-old high school student from Mexico. Joshua considers films to be a way to bring a positive impact to humanity since many of them have positive messages, emotions and facts that can help many people. For this reason, he wants to study cinematography, in particular animation and visual effects. He is passionate about ensuring everyone has a good quality education and proper financial literacy

He enjoys playing stringed instruments, has been a group leader for two years, and is a part of his school's swimming team. Through the life he has had, Joshua has discovered his human vocation so far, which is to volunteer in his school and community to help with problems as needed, such as raising money for non-profit organizations. Thanks to all these activities, he learned the most important values in his life - empathy, responsibility, integrity and commitment

His long-term goal in life is to be happy, as life is short and unpredictable. For this reason, he is the best version of himself every day. Believing that in the end, the only thing that matters is what you did to live your greatest life, and how your heart and soul felt every moment
